Inspired by the Portuguese term "Saudade," which conveys a deep emotional state of nostalgic or profound longing, "Where's your <3" is an immersive exploration of transitional spaces. It invites you to contemplate the complex interplay between physical and emotional presence.

The installation originally located at 808 Gallery at Boston University challenges the notion that our bodies and hearts are always fully present in the same spaces.

This is a space to immortalize people's thoughts and feelings towards longing or nostalgia. Your body may be looking at this screen, but where is your heart really at?

A project by Veridiana Victorelli, 2024
